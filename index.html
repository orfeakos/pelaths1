At Aurum, we believe true wellness is not merely the absence of tension — it is the presence of profound ease. Each visit is a considered journey, not a sequence of appointments.

Our practitioners are trained in the living lineages of Ayurvedic bodywork, Japanese shiatsu, and Mediterranean hydrotherapy, woven together with a contemporary understanding of the nervous system.

The Aurum Way

Your Journey Begins

I 01 Arrival Consultation

Your therapist conducts a private 10-minute wellness consultation to tailor your experience to your body's present needs.

II 02 Thermal Preparation

Begin in our thermal circuit — hammam, sauna, and cold plunge — to prime the body for deep receptor absorption.

III 03 The Ritual Itself

Your chosen treatment, performed in a private suite with curated ambient sound, temperature, and botanical aromas.

A mandatory 30-minute restorative rest in our silence lounge with herbal infusions — the most overlooked half of healing.
